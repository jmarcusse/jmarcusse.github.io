Jordan Marcusse, PhD

CV

I am an economist working on personnel matters related to the Department of Defense. I am part of the Human Capital Group within the Strategy, Forces, and Resources Division at the Institute for Defense Analyses. My work includes applying machine learning to personnel data, developing the full data pipeline from receipt, to cleaning, feature engineering, estimation, analysis, and communicating results to stakeholders across a broad spectrum of technical expertise. My Ph.D. is in Economics, specializing in Macroeconomics and Labor Economics, with both theoretical and structural modeling aspects.
